# Treasure-Trove User Guide

Welcome to Treasure-Trove, a live-auction platform where you can buy and sell items through competitive bidding. This guide will walk you through all the features you need to create an account, list items for auction, place bids, and track your auction history.

## 1. Overview

Treasure-Trove is designed to reward smart buying and selling. Create an account, list items for auction, place bids on items you're interested in, and earn points based on how well you buy and sell relative to estimated item values. Whether you're looking to auction items or find great deals, this platform makes it easy to participate.

## 2. Create Your Account

To get started on Treasure-Trove:

1. Click **Sign Up** in the top navigation bar.
2. Enter a username and password that meet these requirements:
   - At least 7 characters
   - Includes a number
   - Includes a special character (e.g. `!@#$%`)
3. Click **Sign Up**.
   - If your credentials meet the requirements, you'll be redirected to the Login page.
   - You can return to Home at any time using the **back to main** link.

### 2.1. Account & Access

Your account is protected with several security measures:

- Passwords are hashed before storage for maximum security.
- Admins may lock other accounts for inappropriate use, which prevents access to the platform.

## 3. Log In

1. On the Login page, enter your username and password.
2. Click **Log In** to access the main platform.

> **Note:** You must be logged in to access the main platform features.

## 4. After Login (Dashboard Basics)

Once you've logged in, the navigation bar provides quick access to all platform features:

- **Home** – Browse available auctions from other users
- **My Auctions** – View and manage your listed items
- **Purchased Items** – View items you've won
- **Add Auction Item** – Create a new auction listing
- Your username, token balance, and feedback points count appear on the right side
- **Logout** – Sign out of your account

New accounts receive 1,000 free tokens to start bidding.

## 5. Browsing Available Auctions

Once logged in, the Home page displays all available auctions from other users.

- Each auction card shows the item name, description, minimum bid, and time remaining
- Click on any auction card to view full details and place a bid
- Your own auctions do not appear here—see **My Auctions** to manage your listings

## 6. Viewing Auction Details

Click on any auction from the Home page to see:

- Item name and description
- Seller username and posted date
- Countdown timer showing time remaining
- Current highest bid and minimum next bid
- Live bid history that updates in real-time as other users bid

## 7. Placing a Bid

From the auction details page:

1. Click the **Make Bid** button (available only if you have enough tokens).
2. Enter your bid amount (must be at least the minimum next bid).
3. Click **Submit Bid** to confirm.

### 7.1. Bidding Rules

- Your bid must be higher than the current highest bid.
- If there are no bids yet, your bid must meet the minimum bid set by the seller.
- You cannot bid on your own auctions.
- You cannot bid if you're already the highest bidder.
- The auction must still be active (countdown not expired).

## 8. Adding an Auction Item

To list an item for auction:

1. Click **Add Auction Item** in the navigation bar.
2. Fill in all required fields:
   - Item Name – The title of your item
   - Item Description – Details about the item
   - End Date – When the auction closes
   - Minimum Auction Bid – The lowest bid you'll accept
   - Expected Item Value – The item's estimated worth (used for scoring)
3. Click **Submit Auction Item**.
4. You'll be redirected to **My Auctions** upon success.

## 9. Managing Your Auctions

Click **My Auctions** in the navigation bar to view all auctions you've created. From here, you can:

- See the expected value for each of your items
- Delete an auction if needed (removes it from the platform)
- Click on any auction to view its details and bidding activity

## 10. Viewing Purchased Items

Click **Purchased Items** in the navigation bar to see items you've won. For each purchased item, you'll see:

- Item name and description
- Final bid amount (what you paid)
- Expected value (the seller's estimated worth)
- Feedback on whether you got a fair deal or overpaid
- Points earned (+1 for a good deal, -1 if you overpaid)
- Purchase date

Click **View Auction** to see the full auction history.

## 11. Admin Features (Admin Users Only)

Admins can access the Admin Dashboard to manage users on the platform. Admin capabilities include:

- View all users with their username, role, and status
- Lock or unlock accounts (locked users cannot access the platform)
- Manage tokens by adding or removing from user accounts (+100 / -100)

> **Note:** Admin controls are not available for other admin accounts.
