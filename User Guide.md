# Treasure-Trove User Guide

Please follow the directions outlined below to learn how the Treasure-Trove site works.

## 1) Overview
Treasure-Trove is a live-auction platform and when you first load in, you will be able to see the home screen. The home screen is set up in a dark theme to help ease the eye. If you look at the top of the screen, you will see the navigation bar in blue. Within the naviagtion bar you will see the login and signup buttons.

## 2) Create Your Account
1. Click **Sign Up** in the top navigation.
2. Enter a **username** and **password** that meet all requirements:
   - **At least 7 characters**
   - **Includes a number**
   - **Includes a special character** (e.g., `! @ # $ %`)
3. Select **Sign Up**.
   - If your credentials meet the requirements, youâ€™ll be **redirected to the Login page**.
   - You can return to **Home** at any time by using the **back to main** link at the top of the sigup box.

## 3) Log In
1. On the **Login** page, enter your **username** and **password**.
2. Click **Log In** to access the **restricted** (members-only) area.

> **Note:** You cannot access restricted pages unless you sign in.

## 4) After Login (Dashboard Basics)
- **Top Bar:** Your **username** and **token balance** appear in the blue navigation bar.
- **Starter Tokens:** New accounts receive **1,000 free tokens**.
- **Add Tokens:** While the site is in development, you can **add unlimited tokens**.
- **Logout:** A **Logout** option appears next to the token section.

## 5) Account & Access
- **Access Control:** Accounts may be **locked by an admin** for inappropriate use.
- **Password Security:** Passwords are **hashed** before storage.



> **Note:** You will not be able to access this page if you do not sign up and login. You will also be locked out of your account if it is decided by an admin that you have been using this site inappropriately. You can also rest assured that your information is secure since your password is hashed. 

Thank you for using our site and we hope you enjoy learning how to use live auctions.
